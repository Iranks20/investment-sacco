Nyarusiza Investment Club Constitution

1. Introduction

Introduction to NYARUSIZA INVESTMENT CLUB. Purpose and objectives of the club. Reference to foundational and subsequent key meetings.

2. Membership

Criteria: Members should be Bafumbira, or their spouses should be Mufumbira.
Membership fee: UGX 100,000 effective Dec 2023.
Referral process for new members. Joining period:
New members are allowed to join until the 26th week of the financial year. After the 12th week, The first 12 weeks there are no fines.

3. Meetings

Quarterly (every 4 months). Roles and decision-making processes. Fine for missing a meeting is UGX 20,000, unless a valid reason is provided to the chairperson.

4. Savings

Share value is UGX 20,000.
Maximum savings shares per member: 25 (500,000) per week.
Weekly savings period: Friday to Sunday 11:59 PM.
Welfare deduction first before crediting weekly savings.
Payment methods: Directly to the treasurer or approved methods.
Penalty for missing weekly savings: 50% fine. Inclusion of mobile money charges.
Removal from group if no savings sent for 8 weeks at the start of the contribution year, unless communicated otherwise.
Members add withdrawal charges when sending via Mobile money.

6. Welfare Fund

Monthly contribution: UGX 20,000, per member.
Welfare fund to be collected from 1st -7th of every new month.
New members' eligibility: Benefit after 6 months of being a member and contributing to the fund welfare payments.

Allocations:

  1. Childbirth per member parent: UGX 500,000;
  2. Death of a biological dear one: UGX 800,000
  3. Weddings: UGX 500,000 per member.

7. Exiting the Club

Exit procedures.
A written notice to be sent to the chairperson with intent to leave.
Deduction upon exiting: 10% from the payable amount after fines, interest, and penalties.

8. Leadership and Committee Compensation

The club will have a committee of Chairperson, Treasurer, and secretary who will be responsible for running day to day operations of the club. There is also a board that is headed by the chairperson that can help the committee to execute their roles and hold the committee accountable.
Compensation to the committee: 20% of the profits made after relevant expenses or deductions at the end of the year.
