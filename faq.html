Nyarusisa Investment SACCO Constitution

Article I: Name and Objectives

1. Name: The name of this organization shall be Nyarusisa Investment SACCO.

2. Objectives: The objectives of Nyarusisa Investment SACCO shall be to promote thrift among members, create a source of credit, and provide a means for members to save and access financial services.

Article II: Membership

1. Eligibility: Membership is open to individuals who reside in Uganda.

Membership in the SACCO shall be open to any individual who resides in the defined area or meets the specified criteria as outlined in the membership policy.

Article III: Savings

1. Monthly Contributions: Members shall contribute a fixed amount of money on a weekly basis as agreed upon by the SACCO.

Each member is required to contribute 20000 on a weekly basis or a member can decide to increase to any number of shares, and withdrawals are permitted as outlined in the savings policy.

Article IV: Loans and Credit Facilities

1. Loan Facilities: The SACCO may offer credit facilities to members based on established criteria.

The SACCO provides loan facilities to members meeting the specified criteria. Interest rates, repayment terms, and other loan conditions are outlined in the loan policy.

Article V: Investment and Dividends

1. Investment Opportunities: The SACCO may invest funds in specified investment options.

Article VI: Governance and Management

1. Management Committee: The SACCO shall have a management committee elected by the members to oversee its operations.

Article VII: Meetings

1. General Assembly Meetings: Regular meetings of the SACCO shall be held frequently to discuss financial matters, policies, and decisions.

Article VIII: Amendments

1. Amendments: Any amendments to this constitution shall be made by a majority vote at a general assembly meeting, provided that the proposed amendments have been communicated to members in advance.

Article IX: Dissolution

1. Dissolution: In the event of dissolution, assets remaining after settlement of all liabilities shall be distributed among members in proportion to their savings contributions.

Article X: Ratification

This constitution shall come into force upon ratification by a two-thirds majority vote of the members present at a general assembly meeting.

Date: 1st/jan/2024
