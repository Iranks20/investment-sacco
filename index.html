about us

Easy, fee-free investment club for entrepreneurs

Nyarusiza Investment Club (NIC) is a member owned Savings and Credit Cooperative Organization (SACCO). The SACCO was established on the 1st of June, 2020 to enable its members and the general public access affordable alternative financial services in a timely manner. At NIC, we are a family and are committed to holistic personal growth for all our members. Alongside our service offering, we provide a platform where fruitful business synergies can be created through friendships. NIC is here to see you grow and realize your dreams.

Presently, the SACCO has established it’s roots firmly and is set to solve it’s members social and economic problems.
